LESSON #9: PROSMOTR VPERED/NAZAD


1) PROSMOTR VPERED

  =     Ludovic(?=XVI)      - `Ludovic` w/ pattern `XVI`
                            - hide next pattern from results

  !     Ludovic(?!XVI)      - `Ludovic` w/o pattern `XVI`
                            - hide next pattern from results

2) PROSMOTR NAZAD

  <=    (?<=Ludovic)XVI     - `Ludovic` w/ pattern `XVI`
                            - hide first pattern from results


  <!    (?<!Ludovic)XVI     - all `Ludovic` w/o pattern `XVI`
                            - hide first pattern from results
